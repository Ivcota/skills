# Paths to 10/10

For the weakest lever, start at the user's current score and climb one rung at a time. Each rung lists the specific tactic, rough effort, and the score it unlocks. Do not skip rungs — a 3 cannot jump to 9 without passing through 5, 6, 7.

## Dream Outcome — path to 10

| Current | Next move | How | Effort | Unlocks |
|---------|-----------|-----|--------|---------|
| 1-2 | Pick a single avatar | Choose one customer segment. Kill the rest. | 1 day | 3 |
| 3 | Get their words | Interview 10 customers. Write down verbatim phrases for "ideal result." | 1 week | 5 |
| 5 | Quantify it | Attach a number and a time frame to the outcome (revenue, lbs, hours saved). | 2 hours | 6 |
| 6 | Add emotional layer | Rewrite as: "Go from [painful state] to [dream state]." Name the feeling. | 1 hour | 7 |
| 7 | Add identity layer | Write who they become: "the founder who…", "the parent who…" | 1 hour | 8 |
| 8 | Benchmark against best customer result | Use your top 1% outcome as the headline anchor, with proof. | 1 week | 9 |
| 9 | Uniquely articulated | Phrase the outcome in a way no competitor can copy because it's domain-specific + time-specific + emotion-specific. | 1 day | 10 |

**Why it matters:** Dream Outcome is the ceiling on your price. No other lever compensates for a weak "what."

## Perceived Likelihood — path to 10

| Current | Next move | How | Effort | Unlocks |
|---------|-----------|-----|--------|---------|
| 1-2 | Get 3 beta customers free | Run a free pilot in exchange for testimonials and data. | 2-6 weeks | 3 |
| 3 | Ship first 3 case studies | Specific names, metrics, before/after, timeline. Publish. | 1 week | 5 |
| 5 | Name your system | Give your process a proprietary name ("The 3-Step Pipeline Method"). Document the steps. | 1 day | 6 |
| 6 | Add a money-back guarantee | Conditional or unconditional. Put it on the sales page. | 1 day | 7 |
| 7 | Scale to 20+ proof assets | Testimonials + case studies + screenshots + data + credentials. Proof stack. | 1-3 months | 8 |
| 8 | Add a performance guarantee | "If you don't hit X, we keep working free / refund double." | planning + legal | 9 |
| 9 | Publish running metrics | Public dashboard, success rate, total $ generated for customers, etc. | ongoing | 10 |

**Why it matters:** The prospect is always asking "will this work for *me*?" Every rung here lowers that doubt.

| Current | Next move | How | Effort | Unlocks |

**Why it matters:** Speed is a denominator. A lever here moves from 5→8 can double the composite score.

**Why it matters:** Effort is the other denominator. "Done-for-you" is the single biggest price multiplier in business.

## Choosing the rung

- Pick the smallest rung above current score. Do not skip.
- Prefer denominator improvements (Time Delay, Effort) for compounding impact.
- Prefer Dream Outcome fixes if score is below 5 — nothing else matters until the "what" is clear.
- Prefer Perceived Likelihood fixes if the business has customers but isn't converting — the gap is usually trust, not desire.

## First-week action rules

The "this week" action must satisfy all three:

1. **Takes ≤ 5 hours total** — it has to actually happen
2. **Produces a visible artifact** — a doc, a page, a video, a case study, a template
3. **Moves exactly one lever up exactly one rung** — no multitasking across levers

If the user's "week 1 action" doesn't fit all three, downscope it.
