## Coverage estimate

- **Kernel (diagnosis, guiding policy, coherent action):** strong coverage across all secondary sources; direct quotes available.

- **Sources of power (9 total):** Leverage, Proximate Objectives, Chain-link Systems, Design, Focus, Growth, Advantage, Dynamics, Inertia & Entropy — covered across summaries.
- **Case studies:** Apple, Nvidia, Wal-Mart, Desert Storm well-documented; others lighter.
